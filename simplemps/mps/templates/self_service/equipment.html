Hints and Directions

1. Decide on the type of equipment. You may add printers/copiers that you already own (include existing) or select new equipment that will be purchased as part of the proposal.

2. Use the filters to narrow your options. Existing equipment can only be filtered by Brand and Mono/Color while new equipment includes many more options to help you decide what best meets your needs. When you check off an option, this area of the screen will be replaced with a list of equipment that meets the criteria.

3. When you see a model which you would like to add to the proposal, click Select and you will be able to fill in some important information about the device. Lastly click Add when you are finished inputting data.

4. You may switch between types of equipment as much as you like, we will keep track of everything for you! When you switch equipment types, the filters will be cleared and these instructions will be displayed.

Once you have finished selecting one type of equipment, click the Review Selection tab or change the Equipment Type to add a different type. Good luck!



Tips & Tricks:

- As a rule, less expensive equipment costs more per page to print (supplies tend to be more expensive) and they donâ€™t hold up as well under heavy print volumes.

- Color printing is much more expensive than monochrome. Some of the new color printers have a greatly reduced printing cost. If you have an older color device, replacing it with a new color (or monochrome) printer may provide a quick ROI.

- If you need to print 11x17 documents, there are fewer equipment choices and they tend to be higher priced.

- Consider taking this opportunity to replace some of your older devices with newer more reliable printers. While you may have elected to include service with your proposal, down time is still expensive.

- New print technology tends to be much faster than on older devices.
